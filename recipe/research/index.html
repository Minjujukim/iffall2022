How to Make Caramel Custard Pudding

Let's make caramel custard pudding!

Description

5 servings in total

Recipe for caramel:

Ingredients:

  • 80g of sugar
  • 15ml of water

  1. Add 80g of sugar along with 15ml of water in to a small saucepan.
  2. Have the ingredients under low heat since low heat will make the sugar crystalize easier
  3. Pour caramel into moulds

Recipe for pudding:

Ingredients:

  • 3 egg yolk
  • 3 eggs
  • 500ml of milk
  • 90g of sugar
  • 200g of heavy cream
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • Hot water

  1. Have three eggs separated their yolk and the white
  2. Have the 3 yolks stirred with another 3 whole eggs until they combine together
  3. In a pot, add 500ml of milk, 90g of sugar, and 200g of heavy cream
  4. Set the pot on medium heat and stir the misture constantly to dissolve sugar and to prevent the milk from burning
  5. Heat the mixture until you see foaming forming at the edge. Do not boil it!
  6. Add the mixture into the egg mixture previously made. As you pour the milk into the eggs, do it gradually and gently mix
  7. Add 1 tsp of vanilla extract into the mixture
  8. Strain the mixture to remove unmixed eggwhite.
  9. Pour the mixture into the moulds where caramels are hardened
  10. Put the moulds in a deep tray and fill 2/3 of the tray with boiling water
  11. Bake the pudding at 150 celcius degree for 40-50 minutes
  12. After taking it out from the oven, put them in the fridge for 3-4 hours to make them solid
  13. Enjoy!
